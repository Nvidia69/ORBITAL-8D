Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy.

Welcome to Orbital 8D Audio Suite. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do and do not collect, and how we use that information.

Information Collection & Security

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, physical address, or email address unless explicitly provided via support channels.
  • Local Processing: All audio and video files uploaded for processing are handled locally within your browser. Your files are never uploaded to, stored on, or transmitted through our servers.

Cookies & Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies, specifically Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Third-Party Services

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
